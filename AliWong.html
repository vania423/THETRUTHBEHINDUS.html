About Her

Ethnicity: American

Birthday: April 19, 1982

Education: San Francisco University High School, University of California- Los Angeles

Occupation

Alexandra "Ali" Wong is an American actress, stand-up comic, and writer.She is noted for her Netflix stand-up special Baby Cobra, as well as her television appearances in American Housewife, Are You There, Chelsea?, Inside Amy Schumer, and Black Box. She also wrote on the first three seasons of the sitcom Fresh Off the Boat.

Achievements

She was one of the few select female comedians to be "passed" at the Comedy Cellar.
