About Her

Ethnicity: Japanese

Birthday: January 25, 1968

Born in: Tokyo, Japan

Occupation

Aya Kamikawa is a municipal official, the first openly transgender person to seek or win elected office in Japan. She was elected in April 2003.Kamikawa, then a 35-year-old writer, submitted her election application papers with a blank space for "sex.”

Achievements

She won a four-year term as an independent under huge media attention, placing sixth of 72 candidates running for 52 seats in the Setagaya ward assembly, the most populous district in Tokyo.
