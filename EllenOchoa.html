About Her

Ethnicity: Hispanic- American

Birthday: May 10, 1958

Born in: Los Angeles, California

Education: Stanford University

Occupation

Ellen was an American engineer, former astronaut, and the current Director of the Johnson Space Center. Ochoa became director of the center.

Achievemnts

Ellen Ochoa received her master of science and doctorate degrees at Stanford University. She was selected by NASA in 1990 and in 1991 became the world's first Hispanic female astronaut.Ochoa's numerous awards include NASA's Exceptional Service Medal (1997), Outstanding Leadership Medal (1995) and Space Flight Medals (2002, 1999, 1994, 1993).
