About Her

Ethnicity: American

Birthday: August 13, 1860

Born in: Willowdell, Ohio

Occupation

Annie Oakley was an American sharpshooter and exhibition shooter. Her "amazing talent" first came to light when she was 15 years old, when she won a shooting match with traveling-show marksman Frank E. Butler, whom she eventually married.

Achievements

Annie’s sharp shooting in Buffalo Bill's Wild West Show won her many awards and captivated audiences far and wide.
