About Her

Ethnicity: Canadian-American

Birthday: September 26, 1981

Born in: Saginaw, MI

Education: The Art Institute of Fort Lauderdale

Occupation

Melissa Jeanette "Missy" Franklin is a competition swimmer and five-time Olympic gold medalist.

Achievements

She currently holds the world record in the 200-meter backstroke and American records in both the 100-meter and 200-meter backstroke.
