Occupation

Beyonce Knowles’s professions consist of acting, modeling, singing, songwriting, and a composer. She had previously been in an all female music group called Destiny’s Child alongside with Kelly Rowland and Michelle Williams during the late 1990s-early 2000s. Her father Matthew Knowles, was the band manager. They landed their record deal in 1997 to Columbia records. After a while Knowles had began a solo career. Still successfully doing so Beyonce has blown up throughout the music industry, creating recent albums such as Lemonade (2016), B’Day (2006), Drunk in Love (2014), and etc.
